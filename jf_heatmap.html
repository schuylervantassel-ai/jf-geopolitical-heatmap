How to Use
🗺 Navigate the map
Click and drag to pan. Use the scroll wheel to zoom in and out.
🌍 Switch projections
Toggle between Flat and Globe views using the buttons in the top right. On the globe, drag to rotate and scroll to zoom.
🔵 Click a country
Click any shaded country to open a list of articles mentioning it in the sidebar. Darker shading means more coverage.
📰 Browse articles
Hover over article cards to highlight all countries mentioned in that piece. The highlight intensity reflects how central each country is to the publication's overall coverage. Click any card to open the full article.
📚 Switch publications
Use the tabs at the top to switch between All Coverage, Eurasia Daily Monitor, China Brief, and Terrorism Monitor.
⏸ Globe auto-rotation
The globe rotates automatically. Press Space to pause and resume. Drag hard in either direction to flip the rotation direction.
🎧 Sound
Interactive sounds respond to your actions. Use the volume slider or mute button in the bottom-left corner to adjust.
